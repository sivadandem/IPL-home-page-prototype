Orange Army

Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) is called the "Orange Army" because of their signature orange jersey and aggressive play. The team is known for its strong bowling lineup and a loyal fanbase that passionately supports them every season.

Whistle Podu

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) fans chant "Whistle Podu," meaning "blow the whistle" in Tamil. It signifies their excitement, enthusiasm, and unwavering support for the team, led by their legendary captain MS Dhoni.

Ee Sala Cup Namde

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) fans chant "Ee Sala Cup Namde," meaning "This time, the cup is ours" in Kannada. Despite never winning the IPL, RCB boasts a dedicated and passionate fan following.

Paltan

Mumbai Indians (MI) fans are known as the "MI Paltan," meaning "MI Army." With multiple IPL trophies, MI has built a reputation as one of the most dominant and successful franchises in IPL history.

Korbo Lorbo Jeetbo

Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) fans chant "Korbo Lorbo Jeetbo," meaning "We will do, we will fight, we will win." This slogan represents the team's never-give-up attitude and hunger for victory.

Capitals Army

Delhi Capitals (DC) fans call themselves the "Capitals Army," reflecting their loyalty to the team. Over the years, DC has evolved into a formidable side, regularly competing at the highest level.

Halla Bol

Rajasthan Royals (RR) fans use the chant "Halla Bol," meaning "Make Some Noise" in Hindi. This phrase reflects their fearless approach and strong determination to fight till the very end.

Sher Squad

Punjab Kings (PBKS) are called the "Sher Squad," meaning "Lion Squad." The name signifies their aggressive and fearless playing style, aiming to roar louder with every match they play.

Titans Army

Gujarat Titans (GT) fans rally behind the name "Titans Army," symbolizing their team's resilience, strength, and determination to conquer every challenge that comes their way.

Super Giants

Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) fans proudly support their team as the "Super Giants," reflecting their squad's power-packed performances and unyielding spirit on the field.
